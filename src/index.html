Information

  • Minute Ventilation (liters of air breathed per minute) can be accurately estimated based on heart rate(1). The concentration of PM2.5 can be calculated from AQI(2) and combined with exposure time the total PM2.5 mass breathed can be calculated.

  • The calcultor defaults to a male user, but if the female box is checked minute ventilation is reduced to 75%. This is done because on average women have smaller sized lungs than men leading to a potential minute volume of 75% compared to men(1)(2).
